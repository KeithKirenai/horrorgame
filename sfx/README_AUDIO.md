# Audio Assets Guide

This folder is for your FL Studio exports.

## Required Files

### 1. `placeholder_ambience.mp3`
- **Style:** Dark industrial drone, wind, low hum.
- **FL Studio Tips:**
  - Use a synth (like Sytrus or Poizone) with a low-pass filter.
  - Add Reverb (Fruity Reverb 2) with a long decay (4-6s) and high "Wet" level.
  - Export as a 15-30 second loop.
- **Godot Setup:** After copying here, click the file in Godot, go to the **Import** tab, check **Loop**, and click **Reimport**.

### 2. `placeholder_step.mp3`
- **Style:** Concrete or gravel footstep.
- **FL Studio Tips:**
  - Record yourself tapping a desk or crushing paper, or use a noise generator with a short envelope (fast attack, short decay).
  - Add a High-pass filter to remove muddy lows.
  - Keep it short (0.5 seconds).

### 3. `placeholder_scare.mp3`
- **Style:** Loud, jarring noise.
- **FL Studio Tips:**
  - Smash a few keys on a piano plugin (cluster chord).
  - Use "Fruity Squeeze" or Bitcrusher for distortion.
  - Reverse a cymbal crash.
